Ashton, a.k.a. Teeter, was born in Maryland on Feburary 26, 2004 and moved to Texas at the age of 2. He started streaming and got popular on TikTok at the age of 17. Using his effervescent personality he was able to attract a loyal following of supporters.

On June 5, 2023 he came out to his community as a bisexual trans man, but more recently has expressed he's questioning his sexuality and may be asexual. Shortly after coming out he started raising funds to get chest surgery. Unfortunately, he wasn't able to raise enough and turned to sex work in order to get access to healthcare. While he was able to get the healthcare he needed, doing this work led to a severe decline in his mental health. As he put it, "I was using a part of my body I hated and was disgusted by in order to get money, and the only reason I was trying to get money was to get them removed." By the grace of the gods he was able to get enough money to get top surgery on December 28, 2023. With the love and support from firends and family he was able to recover physically and mentally and has been streaming consistentely and looks forward to what the future holds.
